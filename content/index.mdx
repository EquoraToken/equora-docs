# Welcome to Equora (EQR)

Welcome to the official documentation site for **EquoraToken (EQR)**, an open-source project dedicated to advancing equality, equity, and justice through blockchain technology.

EquoraToken is  deployed on the Ethereum network, created with the mission of empowering equal voices and ensuring everyone has a stake in building a fairer future. As our name suggests, we aim to develop a more equitable world where everyone has the opportunity to thrive.

## Join the Movement

EquoraToken thrives on collaboration. Whether you're a developer, advocate, or someone passionate about equity, we invite you to be part of our journey. Together, we can build a fairer world for everyone.

Ready to get started? Dive into the docs or visit our [GitHub](https://github.com/EquoraToken/) to explore the source code and token contract.

Thank you for joining us in advancing equal opportunity and justice for all.
