# Welcome to Equora (EQR)

Hello, world! Welcome to the official documentation site for **EquoraToken (EQR)**, an open-source project dedicated to advancing equality, equity, and justice through blockchain technology.

EquoraToken is a no-fee token deployed on the Ethereum network, created with the mission of empowering equal voices and ensuring everyone has a stake in building a fairer future. As our name suggests, we aim to develop a more equitable world where everyone has the opportunity to thrive.

## Join the Movement

EquoraToken thrives on collaboration. Whether you're a developer, advocate, or someone passionate about equity, we invite you to be part of our journey. Together, we can build a fairer world for everyone.

Ready to get started? Dive into the docs or visit our [GitHub](https://github.com/Equora) to explore the source code.

Thank you for joining us in advancing equality, equity, and justice!
